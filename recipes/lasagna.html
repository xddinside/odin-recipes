Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a beloved Italian comfort dish featuring layers of tender pasta sheets, a rich and savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese filling, and plenty of gooey mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. The recipe typically starts with browning a mixture of ground beef and Italian sausage, then simmering it with tomatoes, tomato paste, and Italian herbs to create a flavorful sauce. Meanwhile, a creamy ricotta mixture is prepared with egg, parsley, and Parmesan for added richness. The dish is assembled by layering the meat sauce, cooked lasagna noodles, and cheese mixture in a baking dish, repeating the layers, and finishing with a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan. Baked until bubbly and golden, lasagna is hearty, cheesy, and perfect for feeding a crowd or enjoying as leftovers

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients - Assemble all components including meats, tomatoes, herbs, cheeses, and lasagna noodles.
  2. Brown meats - Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven until well-browned.
  3. Simmer sauce - Add crushed tomatoes, tomato products, water, and seasonings. Simmer covered for 1½ hours.
  4. Cook noodles - Boil lasagna noodles for 8-10 minutes, then drain and rinse.
  5. Prepare ricotta mix - Combine ricotta cheese with egg, parsley, and salt.
  6. Preheat oven - Heat to 375°F (190°C).
  7. Layer ingredients - Start with meat sauce, then noodles, ricotta mix, mozzarella, and Parmesan. Repeat layers.
  8. Add final cheese layer - Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
  9. Cover with foil - Use greased foil to prevent cheese sticking.
  10. Bake covered - 25 minutes at 375°F (190°C).
  11. Bake uncovered - Remove foil and bake 25 more minutes until bubbly.
  12. Rest before serving - Let stand 15 minutes to set.
