Margherita Pizza

Description

Margherita pizza is a classic Italian pizza that celebrates simplicity and fresh flavors. It features a thin, chewy crust topped with a vibrant tomato sauce, creamy fresh mozzarella cheese, and aromatic basil leaves, all drizzled with extra-virgin olive oil. The colors of the toppings—red, white, and green—reflect the Italian flag. This pizza is baked at a high temperature until the crust is golden and crisp and the cheese is bubbling. Finished with a sprinkle of red pepper flakes or black pepper and more fresh basil, Margherita pizza is beloved for its balance of tangy tomato, milky cheese, and fragrant herbs, making it a timeless favorite

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 pinch white sugar
  • ¼ cup flour for dusting
  • 2 cups pizza sauce
  • 20 slices fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 20 leaves fresh basil
  • olive oil
  • sea salt to taste

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients: Stir flour and 1 teaspoon salt in a bowl. Set aside.
  2. Activate yeast: Mix water, yeast, and sugar in a large bowl. Let stand until yeast forms a creamy foam, about 5 minutes.
  3. Make the dough:
    • Stir half the flour mixture into the yeast mixture until no dry spots remain.
    • Gradually stir in the remaining flour, 1/2 cup at a time, mixing well after each addition.
    • When dough pulls together, turn it onto a lightly floured surface and knead until smooth and elastic, about 8 minutes.
  4. First rise: Lightly oil a large bowl, place dough inside, and turn to coat with oil. Cover with a light cloth and let rise in a warm place (80 to 95°F / 27 to 35°C) until doubled in volume, about 1 hour.
  5. Divide dough: Punch dough down, divide into 4 equal pieces, and form each into a ball.
  6. Preheat oven: Preheat oven with a pizza stone to 500°F (260°C).
  7. Shape and top pizzas:
    • Stretch and pat 1 dough ball into a 10 to 12-inch circle.
    • Place dough on a lightly floured pizza peel.
    • Spread 1/2 cup tomato sauce, leaving about 1 inch from the edge.
    • Arrange 5 slices of mozzarella cheese on top.
    • Place 5 basil leaves over the cheese.
    • Drizzle with 1 tablespoon olive oil and sprinkle with sea salt to taste.
    • Repeat for remaining dough balls.
  8. Bake pizzas: Slide each pizza onto the preheated pizza stone. Bake until cheese is bubbly and crust underside is golden brown, about 5 to 7 minutes.
